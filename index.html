MEET OUR TEAM

  • CHASE LAUTENBACH

    Chase is the co-founder of Street Car Takeover in 2013. Chase cut his teeth running the streets in Nebraska with his buddies, including Cleetus McFarland. Justin and Chase met after doing a smaller event when he was a founder member of 1320 Video. Chase is a huge fan of supercars and regularly races during the SCT events. He handles a lot of the behind the scenes business operations and sponsorships.

  • JUSTIN KEITH

    Justin is the co-founder of Street Car Takeover in 2013. He has been around vehicles and racing since he got started at a local Chevrolet dealership fresh out of high school. Justin is also the owner of the world's fastest 5th Gen Camaro "Nemo" that he regularly races at SCT events. Justin handles most of the racer operations during SCT events and is at home in the staging lanes.

  • CHRIS BAKER

    Chris has been in the automotive performance and aftermarket since 1994. He has a marketing and advertising background and has worked for JEGS and Motorstate Distributing. In 2013 he started an ad agency, Cubic Inch Studios, that specializes in automotive related companies. He is also co-founder of CK Truck Magazine. started in 2020 & GM Scene Magazine.
